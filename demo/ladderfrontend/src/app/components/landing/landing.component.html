About the Tennis Ladder

  • Register: Provide your contact information, times your available to play, and your prefered court to join the ladder
  • Challenge players to play matches at least once a week.
  • Report match scores after winning a match
  • View current standings

Ladder play will begin 5/13/2025 and end 7/13/2025. Register by 5/14/2025 to be included in the first round of matches.

  • Ladder matches will be played based on the USTA 3 set format, where the 3rd set is played as a 10 point tiebreaker. Matches outdoors are played with no time limit. Indoor matches will be limited to 2 hours. If a match is interrupted due to darkness or rain the 2 players will agree upon a time to complete the match and complete it within a week.
  • Both players will be required to bring a can of balls to the match.
  • Players will be required to show up at least 10 minutes early for the match.
  • The winner of the match keeps the can of unused balls and reports the scores.
  • There will be a $20 charge to play in the Ladder and I will collect it when I play each person in the ladder
